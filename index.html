Hi, I'm Joy!

I am passionate about creating technology with marginalized populations in a way that maintains justice and equity.

In the fall I will be starting a PhD in Information Science at Cornell. I'm currently a user researcher and software engineer at Google and I have worked on projects related to what Google refers to as "next billion users." I have also done research and built software with hospitals in India, Ghana, and the US as a Fulbright student researcher, visiting researcher at NYU Abu Dhabi, and co-founder of the Harvard College Developers for Development.
